Interpretation of Results -:

•   A Power Factor close to 1 indicates efficient power usage.
•   Power factor ranges -:
          Good: 0.95 – 1
          Poor: 0.85 – 0.95
          Bad: 0.85 and below
•   Fluctuations in power factor and voltage may suggest variations in the load.

Recommendations for Improving Power Factor:
1.   Install Power Factor Correction Devices such as capacitor banks
2.   Ensure a balanced distribution of electrical loads across all phases.
3.   Monitor and Adjust Inefficient Equipment
4.   Utilize IoT Smart Plug Insights for helpful insights
5.   Regular Maintenance of equipments such as power factor correction devices.
